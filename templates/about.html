Our story

From Kampala to the continent

We started as a small team of engineers doing tax-system integrations in Uganda — the unglamorous plumbing that businesses actually run on. A decade later, that obsession with systems that work has grown into a full-service ICT company: software and SDKs, payments and messaging integration, cloud and AI, cybersecurity, networks and round-the-clock support.

Today we serve governments, hospitals, courts, banks and startups across 12+ African countries — with offices in Kampala and Nairobi, and delivery teams that work wherever the problem is.
